Installation

The Envirofy addon is part of a research project at the University of Oldenburg, the Univeristy of Glasgow, and the Univeristy of Bristol. The aim of our research is to determine which behaviour change techniques support consumers in buying eco-friendly foods. .

Envirofy only works with Tesco.
