Shaping the Future

through Research, Education, and Innovation

I'm Dr Gözel Shakeri. I hold a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction and I have over 10 years of experience at the frontier of human-centered, sustainable digitalisation through my internationally award-winning research and communication skills. More

I founded Shakeri et al., a consultancy working to build a future-ready world through human insight and sustainable innovation.
