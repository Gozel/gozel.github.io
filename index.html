Hi, I am Dr Gözel Shakeri

I am on a mission to empower people and organisations to solve complex problems and drive innovation by leveraging human-centered methods, behavioral science frameworks, and collaborative processes.

With over 10 years of experience in human-centered innovation and a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction I am an internationally award-winning researcher and communicator, as well as a certified educator. More

What I do:

Workshops & Trainings

From co-design sessions to custom trainings, I equip you with tools and methods for creative collaboration, innovative problem-solving, and effective communication.
