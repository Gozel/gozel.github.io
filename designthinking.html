Human-centered Design

My work is grounded in human-centered design — a creative problem-solving framework for leading and engaging with empathy and designing innovative solutions to complex problems.

What is Human-Centered Design (HCD)?

Human-centered design (ISO standard ISO 9241-210:2019), also referred to as design thinking, is a problem-solving process, by being predominantly solution-focused. It is comprised of robust methods and core mindsets that, when practiced together, allow people to collaborate, communicate, and work differently.

It involves discovering and understanding customer needs, defining and identifying opportunities, exploring and developing options, and delivering solutions by building and testing prototypes.
