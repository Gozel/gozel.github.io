Dr Gözel Shakeri

I am a human-centered design consultant, workshop facilitator, and innovation strategist. With a passion for solving complex challenges, I have reimagined how people interact with technology --- from transforming in-car experiences to enhancing collaboration in medical practices --- using proven design methods and practices.

Holding a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) 🔗 from the University of Glasgow, my thesis was shortlisted for the Best Computing Science PhD in Scotland 🔗.

Recognized with an Outstanding Young Scientists Award 🔗 for creatively reshaping and redefining the sustainable shopping experience, I have partnered with industry leaders (e.g., Tesco), government bodies (e.g., Food Standards Agency), and NGOs (e.g., WWF-UK) to drive tangible impact. My expertise has taken me to global platforms, including COP26 🔗, the UN Game Changers Lab 🔗, and the Global Conference for Safety Regulation and Sustainability 🔗, where I have led discussions on innovation and sustainability.

As an award-winning science communicator (both in speech and writing), a certified educator 🔗, and a certified manager 🔗, I have channeled my passion for human-centered design into teaching, serving as a university lecturer on human-centered innovation.

With numerous publications 🔗 and as a frequent speaker 🔗 at international conferences, I’m available for public speaking engagements to inspire and share insights on innovation and design.
